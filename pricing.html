Frequently asked

Where does my data live?

On your machine. API keys, trade journal data, and bot state never leave your device. The activation heartbeat sends only a hashed machine ID and key hash — never personal data or trade history.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or cancel anytime from your PayPal subscription settings. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens after I buy?

You’ll receive a license key by email within minutes. Enter it in the app’s Setup screen and you’re live. Keys validate against our server using only your machine ID hash — results are cached for 24 hours.

What brokers are supported?

Alpaca (live & paper), IBKR, Tradier, Luno, Binance, IG South Africa, and cTrader / Pepperstone. More being added regularly.

Is there a free trial?

Monthly plans include a 7-day free trial with no card required. You can also paper-trade indefinitely without a license.

How is billing handled?

PayPal in USD. Use any major card, your PayPal balance, or PayPal’s Pay Later. Your card provider may apply a currency conversion fee.

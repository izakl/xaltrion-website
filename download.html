Installation

Three steps from download to trading.

1

Download

Grab the latest .exe installer from GitHub Releases. Click the asset named XaltrionSetup-…-windows.exe.

2

Install & activate

Run the installer. On first launch, enter your XALT-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX key on the activation screen. No account needed.

3

Connect & trade

Add your broker and AI keys in Settings. Start in paper mode, confirm everything looks right, then flip live when you’re ready.

⚠️
Windows SmartScreen may warn you
The installer is unsigned during beta. Click “More info” then “Run anyway” to proceed. A signed release is planned for v1.0 GA.

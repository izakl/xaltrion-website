Xaltrion AI, operated by Izak Laubscher, sole proprietor, provides Xaltrion, a local-first AI-powered trading bot. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and the limited circumstances in which information leaves your machine.

The short version

Xaltrion runs entirely on your local machine. We collect nothing about your trading activity, positions, balances, usage patterns, or local data. Your data does not leave your machine except for licence validation and the third-party services you choose to connect.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Xaltrion AI does not collect:

  • Trading data, positions, account balances, or journal history
  • Personal information beyond what you provide for licence fulfilment or support
  • Usage statistics, telemetry, analytics, or behavioural tracking
  • Browsing behaviour or app usage patterns
  • Your API keys, which are stored encrypted on your machine only

2. Data We Do Collect

Licence purchase

When you purchase a licence, we may receive or process your email address (for licence delivery and support) and payment information processed by PayPal. We do not store your full payment card details.

Licence activation

When you activate a licence, we process your licence key and your machine fingerprint — an anonymous hardware hash used to bind the licence to a single machine.

3. External Connections

Xaltrion makes only these external connections:

a. Licence validation — license.xaltrion.ai

Sends your licence key and machine fingerprint. Used to verify your licence is valid and correctly bound. Occurs on startup and periodically during use.

b. Version check — xaltrion.ai/version.json

A standard anonymous HTTP GET request. Used to check whether a software update is available. Can be disabled in Settings.

c. Data feed APIs

Only what each data provider API requires. Uses your own accounts and your own API keys.

d. AI APIs

Market context and prompt content for AI reasoning. Uses your own accounts and your own API keys.

e. Notification webhooks

The trade or status notifications you choose to configure. Delivered to Discord, Telegram, or similar endpoints you configure.

4. Your API Keys

All API keys are stored encrypted on your machine. Your API keys are never transmitted to Xaltrion AI servers and are never intentionally logged.

5. Data Security

Licence validation uses HTTPS. Local secrets are stored using local encryption mechanisms, including Windows DPAPI where applicable. You are responsible for securing your device, OS, user account, backups, and broker credentials.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

If South African privacy law applies to you, including the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you. Contact support@xaltrion.ai.
